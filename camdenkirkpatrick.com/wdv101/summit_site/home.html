Who We Are

Summit Farms is more than just a rescue centerâ€”it's a community of compassionate individuals dedicated to making a difference in the lives of dogs in need. Founded in 2004 by Brad and Janet Harker, Summit Farms Rescue specializes in rescuing Alaskan dog breeds such as Malamutes and Huskies. Our mission is clear: to adopt and save as many dogs as possible while promoting the welfare of all domestic animals. With over 2,620 adoptions and counting, our impact is felt far beyond our shelter walls. We believe in fostering respect, understanding, and compassion for all creatures, and we're committed to educating our community about the importance of responsible pet ownership. At Summit Farms, every dog's story matters, and we invite you to join us in our mission to create a world where every dog has a loving home
